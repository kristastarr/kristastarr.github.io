About Me

Hello world, my name is Krista. I live in Austin, Texas with my dog and my cat where I eat lots of tacos and drink lots of coffee. Besides becoming a devloper, one of my other great ambitions in life is to travel as much as possible. In the words of Alexander Sattler:
I would rather own a little and see the world than own the world and see a little.

I have had the incredibly good luck to take not one but two dream vacations in the last two years! Both trips consisted of a week-long sail abroad a six-cabin catamaran, and were a perfect combination of some of my favorite hobbies (swimming, taking photos, reading, and napping!) all while sailing amongst the beautifully diverse islands of the Carribean.

While I can tell you that all of the islands I visited are breathtakingly beautiful, that's pretty much all I can tell you about them! I decided to use this project as an opportunity to learn a little bit about some of the islands I have visited. The following information is courtesey of Wikipedia:

St. Martin/Sint Maarten

  • Capitol: Phillipsburg
  • Population: 33,609 (according to the 2011 census)
  • Area: 14 sq mi
  • National Language: Dutch and English
  • Currency: Netherlands Antillean guilder

British Virgin Islands

  • Capitol: Road Town
  • Population: 28,054 (2010 census)
  • Area: 59 sq mi
  • National Language: English
  • Currency: US Dollar

US Virgin Islands

  • Capitol: Charlotte Amalie
  • Population: 106,405 (2010 census)
  • Area: 133.73 sq mi
  • National Language: none; the following are spoken: English, English Creole, Spanish, French, and French Creole
  • Currency: US Dollar
