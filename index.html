About Me

Hello world, my name is Krista. I live in Austin, Texas with my dog and my cat where I eat lots of tacos and drink lots of coffee. Besides becoming a devloper, one of my other great ambitions in life is to travel as much as possible. In the words of Alexander Sattler:
I would rather own a little and see the world than own the world and see a little.

I have had the incredibly good luck to take not one but two dream vacations in the last two years! Both trips consisted of a week-long sail abroad a six-cabin catamaran, and were a perfect combination of many of my favorite hobbies (swimming, taking photos, reading, and napping!) all while sailing amongst the beautifully diverse islands in the Carribean and West Indies.

While I can tell you that all of the islands I visited are breathtakingly beautiful, that's pretty much all I can tell you about them!
